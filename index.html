Use this loop every time you sit down with a new topic. It prevents "tutorial hell" — the trap of watching endlessly without retaining anything.

Watch the video or read the chapter. Don't take heavy notes — just grasp the concept.

Follow the instructor exactly. Write the same SQL query. Draw the same market-sizing tree. Your hands must do the work.
40%

Close the tutorial. Open a fresh, unseen problem. Attempt it entirely from scratch. This is where real learning happens.
10%
Write the gap
Note down the one specific thing you got wrong or got stuck on. Review that note before your next session.
